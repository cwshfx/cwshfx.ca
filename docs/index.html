Who are we?

Community Web Solutions (CWS) is a team of skilled computer science students aiming to gain real world experience in web development. CWS is actively collaborating with local businesses in the Halifax Regional Municipality to strengthen their online presence, and to solidify their work with a professional website. We take much pride in our work and hope you also appreciate it enough to work with us!
